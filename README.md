## <img src="https://api.iconify.design/lucide:user.svg?color=%2306b6d4" width="28" align="top" /> About

I am Tanmaya Mahapatra, also known as Retired AME, an 11th-grade Science student and the Lead Architect & Founder of Drona Labs. I am a self-driven technocrat dedicated to bridging the gap between low-level systems programming and modern, AI-integrated software solutions. My journey is defined by a deep curiosity for computer science fundamentals, balanced with the rigorous demands of competitive academic preparation.

Behind the scenes, I manage a heavy workload of 11 repositories, the majority of which are private systems powering Drona Labs' infrastructure. My public-facing work focuses on two key projects: my custom compiler language, **Vortex Lang**, and my active open-source contributions to the **Liber Kernel (KernelSU integrated dev)** community. Whether I am delving into custom Android kernel development, architecting low-level cross-platform autonomous agents, or optimizing cloud-native infrastructure, I thrive at the intersection of complex algorithms and real-world implementation. 

Beyond software architecture, I actively research cybersecurity—probing systems to uncover and patch vulnerabilities, ensuring the technology I interact with is fortified against exploits. I am committed to building performant, scalable, and secure solutions that solve actual user problems, merging my academic foundation in Science with a professional-grade passion for systems engineering and innovation.

Outside of the terminal, I am a part-time video editor and an active member of the **Toji Motion** Telegram group. I specialize in crafting dynamic visual edits using Alight Motion, CapCut, After Effects, and various other creative suites, bringing the same level of precision to visual storytelling as I do to my code.

**Open To:** Architecture consulting, strategic open-source collaborations, and high-impact technical opportunities.
